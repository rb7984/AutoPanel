dir:
> FORMAT: dir-0b-border
          dir-0v-2.2
          dir-0h-2.2
> polylines in dir-0b-border MUST have Z-coordinate equal to that of baseline

Obstacles:
> ALWAYS 5 LAYERS OF OBSTACLES
> windos must touch nerby panels
> horizontal obstacles must start/finish on the border line
> walls obstacles must start/finish on the border line
> walls will produce panels with the same border

baseline:
> polylines have to be drawn and referenced by hand. Draw them in reverse order (Rhino remembers the last)
